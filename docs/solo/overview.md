# VATSSA Solo Cert Policy

**Version:** 1.2  
**Valid from:** 16 June 2022

> _This policy should be read in conjunction with any applicable VATSIM policy._

---

## 1. Overview

- **Solo Certs** are permissions granted for controllers to operate **one level above** their current rating (specifically for **S2**, **S3**, and **C1** ratings) to gain practical experience prior to a Competency Practical Test (CPT).
- **Not available** for **S1** ratings.

## 2. Conditions & Limits

1. Solo Certs are limited to **one specific position at a time** and may be granted for up to **30 days**.
2. A controller may receive Solo Certs on a maximum of **three separate occasions** before a mandatory CPT.
3. Controllers must be **at examination standard** before being issued a Solo Cert. The purpose of the Solo Cert is to provide **network experience** rather than assessment.

## 3. Issuance Authority

- Solo Certs may **only** be issued by the **ATC Training Manager** or the **Training Centre Manager**.
