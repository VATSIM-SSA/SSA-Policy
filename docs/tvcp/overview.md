# VATSSA Transfer and Visiting Policy

## 1. VATSIM Policies

- This policy should be read in conjunction with any applicable VATSIM policy.

## 2. Definitions

- **Transfer**: A person wishing to permanently transfer to another Region or Division so that their relationship with the old Division ceases and the new Division becomes their registered home Division.
- **Visiting Controller**: A person who wishes to control in another Division whilst retaining the permanent relationship with their existing home Division.

## 3. Transfers to VATSSA

### 3.1 Eligibility & Restrictions (by rating)

- **OBS rating**
  - A VATSIM member may transfer to VATSSA with an OBS rating as long as they have not transferred Region or Division within the last 90 days.
- **S1 or above**
  - A VATSIM member may transfer to VATSSA with a controller rating of S1 or above as long as they have not transferred Region or Division within the last 90 days.
  - A member may only transfer when they have held their rating for **90 days or more**.
  - A member may only transfer when they have controlled **50 hours** towards their current rating, at least **50%** of which must be at the **home Division**.
- **Instructor ratings**
  - Instructor ratings are **not transferrable**. Should a VATSIM member transfer to VATSSA, they will lose their instructor rating and revert to their previously held rating.

### 3.2 Processing & Possible Rejection

VATSSA must accept all reasonable transfer requests and process them by the most expeditious means. An application may be rejected if:

- The member is not eligible for transfer.
- The member has documented history which must be taken into consideration.
- At the discretion of VATSSA staff with good reason and the right to appeal to Division and Region staff by the member.

### 3.3 How to Request a Transfer (from a non-EMEA Division)

- Visit **my.vatsim.net** and request to change Region.

### 3.4 How to Request a Transfer (within EMEA)

- Contact the **VATSSA Membership Manager** through Discord, or by email (address available on **vatssa.com**).

### 3.5 Post-Transfer Competency Check

- Newly transferred controllers who did not previously hold controlling approvals within VATSSA shall undertake a **competency check** with an approved mentor, as per the Training Guide and Policy.
- If VATSSA controlling standards are not met within **90 days**, or if the member does not accept the local induction plan, the transfer becomes **void** and they may return to their previous Division **without loss of their current rating**.

## 4. Visiting Controllers

### 4.1 Eligibility

- Hold a VATSIM **ATC rating of S2 or above**.
- The rating must have been held for **90 days or more**.
- The rating must have been used to control for at least **50 hours in the home Division** on positions relevant to that rating.

### 4.2 Instructor Restrictions

- A visiting Controller shall **not** control using any **instructor rating** that may be held.
- Instructor ratings may **not** be granted to visiting Controllers.
- Visiting Controllers **may** apply to become **mentors** at VATSSA.

### 4.3 Processing

- VATSSA must accept all reasonable visiting Controller requests and process them by the most expeditious means.

### 4.4 How to Apply

- Refer to the **VATSSA Training Guide and Policy** to request Controller training.

### 4.5 Grounds for Rejection

- The member is not eligible to visit VATSSA.
- The member has documented history which must be taken into consideration.
- At the discretion of VATSSA staff with good reason and the right to appeal to Division staff by the member.
