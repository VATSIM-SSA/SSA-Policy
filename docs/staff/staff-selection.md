# VATSSA Policy on Staff Selection Process

**Version:** 1.0  
**Valid from:** 1 February 2026

> _This document should be read in conjunction with any applicable VATSIM policy._

---

## 1. Purpose

- This document outlines the current VATSSA Staff selection process. 
- VATSSA Staff work to ensure that our Staff selection process has been carefully thought out and is followed to the best of our ability to ensure that no candidate is in receipt of any bias or favoritism throughout their application. 
- **VATSSA reserves the right to reject any applicant we feel unsuitable for the role.**

## 2. Roles and Responsibilities

### 2.1 Division Director `(VATSSA1)`

- Directs Staff team recruitment
- Approves Staff team recommendations
- Identifies Staff team needs
- Conducts all interviews

### 2.2 Deputy Division Director `(VATSSA2)`

- Reports to VATSSA1
- Identifies Staff team needs
- Schedules all interviews
- Recommends suitable candidates to `VATSSA1`
- Posts Vacancies and Advertisements

## 3. Vacancy Identification

Staff team vacancies are identified by `VATSSA1` and `VATSSA2`.

## 4. Advertisement Process

- Vacancies shall be advertised in 2 locations
  1. Discord - Staff Openings Channel
  2. VATSIM Vacancies Forum

- All advertisements shall include a suitable role description enclosed as a separate document
  
- **Required Items**
  1. Position title
  2. Discord title
  3. Reporting Line
  4. Key roles and responsibilities, including general Staff responsibilities.
  5. Suitable skills and experience
  6. How to apply and Closing date
- **Optional Items**
  1. Decision timeline
  2. Team size and structure
  3.  Optional responsibilities
  4.  Available Resources
  5.  Supporting team members / Responsibilities
   
> _This document should be read in conjunction with any applicable VATSIM policy._For all senior Staff positions VATEMEA will be notified by the appropriate Staff member._

## 5 . Application Process

- Applicants wishing to apply for any advertised roles are to submit their applications prior to the closing date to the email provided.
- **VATSSA reserves the right to reject any application that is submitted late.**
- To eliminate bias and favouritism all applicants will have their application reviewed by both ``VATSSA1` and `VATSSA2` independently. It may also be reviewed by a suitable 3rd member of Staff.
- All applicants who successfully make it through the initial filter stage will be invited to a face to face interview with at least 2 suitable members of Staff.
- Post interview, every applicant will be assessed by VATSSA1 and VATSSA2. They may also be assessed by a suitable 3rd member of Staff where relevant.
- Applicants will be assessed on multiple criteria, all of which is below in the Decision Process.
- The successful applicant will then be contacted by either VATSSA1 or VATSSA2 to arrange their arrival into the Staff team.

## 6. Decision Process

- All applicants will be reviewed by `VATSSA1` and `VATSSA2`.
- Applicants will be evaluated based on multiple criteria. These include but are not limited to:
  1. C.V
  2. Suitable Skills
  3. Interview Performance
  4. Staff team recommendation
  5. Current and prior experience
  6. Current behavioural standing within the division
  7. Prior contribution to the division
- All applicants have a right to withdraw their application at any stage.
- All applicants have a right to request an update of their application at any stage in the decision process.
- All applicants will be notified of the outcome of their application.

## 7. Team - Operations

- Operations team appointments are at the discretion of `VATSSA1` and `VATSSA2`.
- People may become eligible for the Operations Team if a current Staff member provides a statement on their behalf

## 8. Mitigation of Bias or Favouritism

- All applicants are subject to a face to face interview, provided they pass the initial filter stage.
- Applications shall be read in no particular order and in conjunction with this policy.
- All applicants will be reviewed by both `VATSSA1` and `VATSSA2` independently.
- Where applicable an existing 3rd member of Staff may evaluate candidates.
- Applicants are chosen based solely on their ability to fulfill the job requirements, independent of any internal referrals or relationships.
