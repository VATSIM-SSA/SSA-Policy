# VATSSA Staff Policy

**Valid from:** 16 June 2022

---

## 1. VATSSA Staff

### 1.1 Staff Roles

The following are the official VATSSA staff roles:

- Division Director (`VATSSA1`)
- Deputy Director (`VATSSA2`)
- ATC Training Manager
- Pilot Training Manager
- Membership Manager
- Webmaster
- Events Manager
- Sector File Manager
- Documentation Manager

### 1.2 Appointments

- The **Division Director** is appointed by the **EMEA Region Director**.
- All other staff positions are appointed by `VATSSA1` and can remain vacant if necessary.

### 1.3 ATC Training Manager Requirements

- Must be a controller holding a **C1 rating or higher**.

### 1.4 Pilot Training Manager Requirements

- Must be a pilot holding a **PPL rating or higher**, or possess **equivalent real-world qualifications**.

### 1.5 Data Protection Compliance

- All staff must be fully familiar with the **VATSSA Data Protection and Handling Policy**.
- Staff must agree to comply with this policy at all times.

### 1.6 Conduct and Representation

- All staff are expected to **represent VATSSA at all times**.
- Any report of misconduct will be investigated accordingly.
