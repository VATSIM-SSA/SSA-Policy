# VATSSA Currency Policy

**Valid from:** 15 June 2022

> _This document should be read in conjunction with any applicable VATSIM policy._

---

## 1. Currency Requirements

- **Home Controllers** must control at least **3 hours per quarter** within the Division to maintain currency.  
  (Quarters: Jan–Mar, Apr–Jun, Jul–Sep, Oct–Dec)
- **Visiting Controllers** must also control at least **3 hours per quarter** within the Division to maintain currency.  
  (Same quarters apply)
- **Overload FSS positions** (named `AFRx_FSS`) require **1 hour per quarter** within the Division to maintain currency.  
  (Same quarters apply)

---

## 2. Consequences of Non-Compliance

- Failure to meet the above currency requirements may result in **withdrawal of controlling approvals** within the Division **without notice**.
- The only recognized source for current approvals is the listing on **vatssa.com**. **No other source** will be accepted as proof of approval to control.

---

## 3. Unauthorized Operations

- If a controller logs into a position **without the required approvals**, a **VATSIM Supervisor may be contacted**.
- Supervisors can access the current list of approvals on **vatssa.com**, and Division staff will follow up as necessary.
